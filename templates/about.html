About Us

We help extra food reach people instead of landfills.

FoodShare is designed for fast, practical coordination between donors and NGOs. The platform reduces waste, shortens response time, and gives administrators clear visibility into every donation cycle.

Mission

Reduce hunger and food wastage by making donation workflows simple, trackable, and community-driven.

Vision

Create a city-wide digital network where every excess meal has a direct path to a family, shelter, or relief center.

Approach

Use role-based dashboards, donation tracking, and a centralized admin panel to keep coordination smooth and accountable.
